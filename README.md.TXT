
Using the World Bank geo-coded Project data, exploratory visualisation analysis has been done to analyse the project to eradicate or 
significantly reduce the poverty across the world by 2030. In Subpart 1, The Tableau has been chosen as a platform to do so. Tableau 
offering a wide range of variety of options to develop the visualisation but are complex in nature. The dataset has been loaded and 
inner joined and left at extract mode than live mode.

In subpart 2, The PowerBI paltform has been used to explore the dataset. Its offering wide interaction abilities than tableau.

The datasets are joined in RapidMiner software.
